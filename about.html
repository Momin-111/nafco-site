Our Story

A half-century of aluminium engineering in the Kingdom

National Aluminium Factory Company NAFCO was established in 1971 in the Second Industrial Area of Riyadh. What began as a regional manufacturer has grown into one of Saudi Arabia's leading forces in aluminium fabrication, kitchens, and metal coating.

Today, NAFCO operates seven fully integrated specialized factories employing over 1,300 engineers, fabricators, and skilled professionals. Our team brings decades of hands-on experience to every project precision curtain walls, structural facades, high-rise cladding, and complex aluminium systems that define the modern Saudi skyline.

With deep roots in Riyadh's industrial landscape and a proven track record across the Kingdom's most ambitious infrastructure projects, NAFCO stands as the trusted partner for clients who demand engineering-grade performance on technically complex builds.

Facade & Curtain Wall Systems

Quality Management

Growth & Development

Continuously expanding our capabilities

NAFCO's leadership team actively drives strategic development across all operations opening new markets, modernizing production, and investing in infrastructure to stay ahead of Saudi Arabia's growing demand for complex aluminium systems.

01
European & American System Adoption
Integrating international aluminium systems from European and American markets into our production lines, bringing global best practices to Saudi projects.
02
Expanded Coating Capacity
Major expansion of the powder coating factory with new furnaces and updated coating systems, scaled to match growing project volumes across the Kingdom.
03
International Kitchen Markets
Established active export channels for aluminium kitchens beyond Saudi Arabia, with Yemen representing an initial breakthrough market.
04
New Factory & Warehouse Construction
Currently constructing a new dedicated kitchen factory alongside 8,003 m² of new warehousing to accommodate the company's growing material requirements.
05
Company-Owned Worker Accommodation
Built and owns dedicated residential facilities for NAFCO's workforce supporting retention of skilled specialists and ensuring operational continuity.
